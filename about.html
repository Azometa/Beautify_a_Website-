Meet Francine “Fran” Miller

Francine “Fran” Miller opened Fran’s Flowers in 1995 with one mission: to brighten people’s days through flowers and community connection.

What began as a small neighborhood shop grew into a local favorite known for fresh seasonal blooms, friendly service, and thoughtful arrangements.
